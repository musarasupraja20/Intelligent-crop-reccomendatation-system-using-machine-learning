Our Methodology

This project leverages three high-performance algorithms as per research standards:

K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)

Classifies crops based on historical soil similarities.

Support Vector Machine (SVM)

Creates optimal boundaries for multi-dimensional crop data.

Random Forest (99% Accurate)

An ensemble model that provides the final high-precision recommendation.
